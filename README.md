# 🔐 Cybersecurity GRC Risk Register

> A practical Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) framework 
> including a cybersecurity risk register template and analysis.

---

## 🎯 Project Goal

Bridge my audit background with cybersecurity by building a 
GRC framework and risk register that identifies, assesses, 
and manages cyber risks in an organization.

---

## 🛠️ Frameworks & Standards

- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework**
- **ISO 27001 / 27005**
- **COBIT 2019**
- **CIS Controls**

---

## 📊 What's Included

- ✅ Risk Register template (Excel/CSV)
- ✅ Risk assessment methodology
- ✅ Threat and vulnerability mapping
- ✅ Risk scoring (likelihood × impact)
- ✅ Control effectiveness assessment
- ✅ Risk treatment plans
- ✅ Compliance mapping
